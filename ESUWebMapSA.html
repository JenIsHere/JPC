The Study Area

Since the beginning of the project, our client at Ontario Headwaters Institute (OHI) had identified the study area to be within the Greater Golden Horseshoe (GGH) region.

Upon further discussions, the study area was designated to be the Golden Horseshoe region.

The map visualizes boundaries of the municipalities that govern within this region as well as the Greenbelt.

The lands that fall between the existing urban boundaries and the Greenbelt are also visualized on the map, which will be termed as "The Contested Countryside" throughout the project.

The Greenbelt being shown is further classified into various designations such as Niagara Escarpment, Oak Ridges Moraine Conservation Plan, Urban River Valley and the Protected Countryside.

As per A Place to Grow-Growth Plan, the municipalities of GGH are required to provide their development plans through 2051 by the end of June 2022.
