The Study Area

As per A Place to Grow: Growth Plan, the municipalities of Greater Golden Horseshoe are required to provide their development plans through 2051 by the end of June 2022.

The study area of interest to our the OHI was identified to be the municipalities within the Golden Horseshoe (GH) region.

Upon further discussions, the key area of interest within the GH was the "Contested Countryside"

The Contested Countryside is the lands that fall between the existing urban development boundaries from the south and the interior Greenbelt Boundaries

The map visualizes boundaries of the municipalities that govern within this region as well as the Greenbelt.

Navigate through the tabs above to view the results of a Site Suitability Analysis which details the lands at risk in the Contested Country side, as well as the lands most suitable for increased development within the existing urban boundaries.
