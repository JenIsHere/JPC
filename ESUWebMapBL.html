The Balanced Development Approach

As a part of this project, the team performed spatial analyses on the study area to generate site suitability layers.

A major aspect of the analyses was to implement various approaches with varying stances towards the urban development into the Contested Countryside.

This map visualizes the study area as a result of such an analysis, done by following the Balanced Development Approach.

This approach tries to strike a middle-ground between the two extreme stances of urban development and conservation, placing enough importance on the natural heritage while also allowing space for urban growth.

The areas symbolized on the map represent the areas which would be more or less suitable for this type of growth tactic.
